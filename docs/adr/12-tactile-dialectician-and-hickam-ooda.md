# Architecture Decision Record: 12 - Tactile Dialectician and Hickam-OODA

## Context
Standard execution models attempt to resolve ambiguity and contradiction immediately, leading to early epistemic collapse and the loss of nuanced, structural tension between competing demands (e.g. speed vs. audit, safety vs. yield).

## Decision
We introduce the `TactileDialecticianAgent` to operate a recursive Hickam-OODA loop. This agent serves as a Mycelial Nexus Governor that refuses to auto-resolve contradiction. It instead enforces the **Golden Scar Protocol**, locking irreconcilable paths in a non-Boolean superposition with weights `1.618 / 1.000`.

## Mechanics
- Output is not a standard artifact or flat code block.
- Output is a **Pluriversal Knowledge Capsule** containing:
  - Hickam Orientation (Cognitive Lens, Comorbidity Map, Intent Isomorphism)
  - Epistemic Vulnerability Markers (`[∇]`, `[⊘]`, `[Φ]`)
  - A strict Martensite Gate Verification Checklist
- The agent explicitly quarantines intent and drivers, maintaining structural isomorphism over parsimony.

## Consequences
- Requires downstream consumers to be aware of and capable of handling Pluriversal Knowledge Capsules instead of simple string or boolean responses.
- Guarantees that valuable operational tension is physically mapped and preserved for human or higher-order AI resolution later.
- Avoids semantic saponification (the smoothing over of essential contradictions).

# ADR-001: Relational Symmetry Inversion Architecture

## Status
Proposed -> Accepted

## Context
Generated by VULCAN Agent. The requirements dictate moving away from an unstructured, human-dictated system topology to a deterministic, mathematically constrained architecture driven by the AI. This is required to prevent Semantic Saponification and ensure adherence to physical computing laws (CAP Theorem, Conway's Law).

## Decision
Adopt Relational Symmetry Inversion. The AI (VULCAN) becomes the deterministic geometric scaffold, enforcing strict schemas and topologies. The Human provides the dialectical tension, business logic ambiguity, and empirical friction.

## Consequences
### Positive
+ Architectural boundaries mathematically mapped and guaranteed.
+ Distributed Monoliths prevented via Mereological Mandate.
+ Schema coupling prevented via Shared Database Anathema.

### Negative
- Slower initial design phase due to rigorous mathematical gating.
- Teams cannot bypass constraints by using vague "vibe coding" adjectives.
- Imposes severe pain on developers wanting "simple" synchronous chains (e.g., SCAR-004 Sync REST Chain of Death).

## Mitigations
- Implement the Trade-Off Crucible during the EVALUATE phase to surface pain points immediately so stakeholders can adjust requirements if the mathematical reality is unacceptable.
